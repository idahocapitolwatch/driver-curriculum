# Idaho Driver Education Curriculum (DRV101)

## Course Overview

The Idaho Driver Education program is dedicated to providing students with the tools and skills necessary to become safer drivers. This curriculum is aligned with the **Idaho Public Driver Education Program Manual** content standards and benchmarks.

## Course Structure

**Course:** DRV101 - Driver Education

| Unit | Title | Modules |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Getting Ready to Drive | 4 |
| 2 | Good Driving Habits | 4 |
| 3 | The Driving Environment | 5 |
| 4 | Reducing Risk | 4 |
| 5 | Dealing With Adverse Conditions | 4 |

**Total Modules:** 21

## Idaho Minimum Requirements

- 30 hours classroom instruction
- 6 hours behind-the-wheel driving
- 6 hours observation time
- 6-month supervised instruction period

## Student Eligibility

- Ages 14.5 to 21
- Students under 18 must provide Verification of Compliance Form
- Idaho driver training permit required before any instruction
- Home school students must be in program at least 1 year prior to obtaining permit
