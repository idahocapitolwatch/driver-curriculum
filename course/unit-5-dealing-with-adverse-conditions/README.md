# Unit 5: Dealing With Adverse Conditions

## Idaho Content Standards

- 2.4 Preparing to Drive
- 2.5 Protecting Occupants
- 7.4 Driving at Night and in Other Reduced Visibility Conditions
- 7.5 Driving During Adverse Weather Conditions
- 8.2 Responding to Emergencies
- 8.3 Responsibilities after a Crash

## Unit Objectives

By the end of this unit, students will be able to:

1. Identify risks associated with and strategies to compensate for reduced visibility driving conditions
2. Identify risks associated with and strategies to compensate for driving during adverse weather conditions
3. Explain ways to minimize the risk of vehicle failures
4. Identify ways to avoid a collision and, if necessary, minimize the damage of a collision
5. Explain the requirements for what to do after a collision
6. Demonstrate or discuss safe procedures for changing a flat tire
7. Discover connections between classroom learning and behind-the-wheel application
