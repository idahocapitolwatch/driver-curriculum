## Key Content Topics

### Fields of Vision
- Central vision (3 degrees - sharp focus)
- Peripheral vision (up to 180 degrees)
- How each is used while driving
- Scanning patterns and eye movement

### Speed and Vision
- Tunnel vision effect at higher speeds
- Reduced peripheral awareness
- Need for increased visual lead time at speed
- Relationship between speed and reaction distance

### Vision Improvement Techniques
- Aim high in steering (look far ahead)
- Keep eyes moving
- Get the big picture
- Leave yourself an out
- Make sure they see you
- Selective search pattern

### Vehicle Control Sequence
- See (visual input)
- Think (mental processing)
- Do (physical response)
- Vision leads motion and steering

### Optical Illusions
- Speed perception errors
- Distance estimation errors
- Road condition illusions
- Night driving visual challenges
- Effects of fatigue on vision
