## Key Content Topics

### Idaho Graduated Driver Licensing (GDL) Program
- Purpose and goals of the GDL program
- Three stages: supervised instruction permit, restricted license, full license
- Time requirements at each stage
- Compliance procedures

### Course Requirements
- 30 hours classroom instruction
- 6 hours behind-the-wheel driving
- 6 hours observation time
- 6-month supervised instruction period
- Course policies and rules

### Driver Training Permit (Class D)
- Eligibility requirements (ages 14.5-21)
- Application process
- Permit restrictions
- Supervision requirements

### Types of Licenses and Permits
- Instruction permits
- Restricted driver's license
- Full driver's license
- Special endorsements and restrictions
- Information on license (organ donor, veteran, etc.)

### Maintaining Driving Privileges
- License renewal process
- Restrictions, suspensions, and revocations
- Point system and violations
- Reinstatement procedures

### Roles and Responsibilities
- Instructor responsibilities
- Parent/guardian responsibilities
- Student responsibilities
- Purpose and use of the Supervised Driving Guide/Log
