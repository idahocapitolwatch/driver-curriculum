- 6.2 Negotiating Hills and Curves

## Unit Objectives

By the end of this unit, students will be able to:

1. Explain the Idaho Graduated License requirements
2. Explain how to manage the space margin around your vehicle
3. Explain the use of a selective search pattern to minimize risk
4. Explain how gravity, energy of motion, and other natural laws affect a vehicle
5. Explain multiple ways to communicate with other drivers
6. Discover connections between classroom learning and behind-the-wheel application
