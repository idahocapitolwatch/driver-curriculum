## Key Content Topics

### Space Management System (SIPDE/Zone Control)
- Search: Identify potential hazards
- Identify: Determine if hazards are real threats
- Predict: Anticipate what may happen
- Decide: Choose the best action
- Execute: Carry out the decision

### Six Zones Around the Vehicle
- Front zone
- Left-front zone
- Right-front zone
- Left-rear zone
- Right-rear zone
- Rear zone
- Monitoring and maintaining open zones

### Vehicle Positioning and Reference Points
- Blind areas identification (front, sides, rear)
- Front bumper placement references
- Right and left tire placement references
- Center lane positioning
- Rear bumper references
- Turn setup and execution reference points

### Lane Position and Speed Adjustments
- Five lane positions and when to use each
- Speed adjustment for space management
- Responding to line of sight restrictions
- Responding to path of travel restrictions
- Safe gap selection for maneuvers

### Following Distance
- 3-second following rule (minimum)
- Increasing following distance for conditions
- Measuring following intervals
- Tailgating dangers

### Communication Techniques
- Turn signals
- Brake lights
- Horn
- Headlights (flash)
- Eye contact
- Hand signals
- Vehicle positioning as communication

### Consequences of Driving Errors
- Idaho crash statistics
- National crash statistics
- Common driver errors leading to crashes
- How risk can be minimized through good habits
