## Key Content Topics

### Four Types of Distractions
- **Visual:** Taking eyes off the road
- **Manual/Physical:** Taking hands off the wheel
- **Cognitive/Mental:** Taking mind off driving
- **Auditory:** Sounds that divert attention from driving
- How multiple distraction types can overlap (e.g., texting = all four)

### Technology Distractions
- Cell phones and smartphones (texting, calling, apps)
- GPS and navigation systems
- Infotainment systems
- Earbuds and headphones
- Idaho laws regarding mobile device use while driving

### Passenger Distractions
- Conversations with passengers
- Passenger behavior and peer pressure
- GDL passenger restrictions for new drivers
- Managing passenger distractions

### Personal Habits and Conditions
- Eating and drinking while driving
- Grooming or personal care
- Reaching for objects
- Daydreaming
- Emotional states affecting focus
- Outside distractions (billboards, events, scenery)
- Inside vehicle conditions (radio, climate, pets)

### Distraction Reduction Strategies
- Put phone away or on silent/do not disturb
- Set GPS before driving
- Pull over for complex tasks
- Establish passenger rules
- Pre-set radio and climate controls
- Focus on driving as the primary task
- Take breaks on long trips

### Idaho and National Distracted Driving Statistics
- Idaho crash data related to distracted driving
- National crash and fatality statistics
- Teen driver distraction rates
- Economic costs of distracted driving crashes
