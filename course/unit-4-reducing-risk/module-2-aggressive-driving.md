## Key Content Topics

### Aggressive Driving Behaviors
- Tailgating / following too closely
- Speeding and racing
- Weaving through traffic
- Running red lights and stop signs
- Failure to yield right of way
- Improper lane changes
- Failure to signal
- Blocking passing lanes
- Honking excessively
- Flashing headlights aggressively

### Emotions and Driving
- How anger, frustration, and stress affect driving
- Triggers for aggressive driving
- Recognizing emotional states that impair judgment
- Situational factors (running late, traffic, bad day)

### From Aggressive Driving to Road Rage
- Escalation cycle: aggressive driving to road rage
- Road rage behaviors (verbal, physical, vehicular assault)
- Legal consequences of road rage
- Real-world examples and consequences

### Conflict Reduction Strategies
- Don't take other drivers' actions personally
- Allow extra time for trips
- Practice patience and courtesy
- Avoid eye contact with aggressive drivers
- Don't respond to provocations
- Move away from aggressive drivers
- Pull over and take a break if feeling angry
- Report dangerous drivers to authorities
- Be a courteous driver yourself

### Idaho Law and Aggressive Driving
- Legal definition and penalties
- Relationship between aggressive driving and crashes
- Impact on insurance and driving record
