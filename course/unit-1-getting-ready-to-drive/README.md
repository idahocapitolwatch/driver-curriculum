# Unit 1: Getting Ready to Drive

## Idaho Content Standards

- 2.1 Identifying Gauges and Warning Symbols Inside a Vehicle
- 2.2 Operating Vehicle Control Devices
- 2.3 Preparing to Drive
- 2.4 Protecting Occupants
- 2.5 Understanding Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS) Safety Features
- 3.1 Traffic Control Devices and Laws
- 3.2 Right of Way Rules
- 4.1 Vehicle Positioning
- 4.2 Performing Basic Control
- 5.2 Time and Space Management Systems and Strategies
- 5.3 Intersections
- 8.1 Driving Within the Highway Transportation System

## Unit Objectives

By the end of this unit, students will be able to:

1. Identify the components of the Highway Transportation System
2. Identify vehicle equipment and safety features
3. Identify intersection types and apply rules of right of way for each
4. Interact with other class participants to develop a culture of collaboration
