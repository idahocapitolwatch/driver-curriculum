## Lesson Objectives

1. **Identify different intersection types and safe navigation of each**
   - Correlations: 5.3.1, 5.3.2, 5.3.3, 5.3.4, 5.3.5, 5.3.6

2. **Describe appropriate driver responses to roadway signs, traffic signals, and roadway markings**
   - Correlations: 3.1.1, 3.1.2, 3.1.3, 3.1.4, 3.1.5

3. **Identify rules of yielding the right of way in varying situations**
   - Correlations: 3.2.1, 3.2.2, 3.2.3, 3.2.4, 3.2.5, 3.2.6, 3.2.7, 3.2.8

## Key Content Topics

### Intersection Types
- Controlled intersections (signals, signs)
- Uncontrolled intersections
- T-intersections
- Multi-lane intersections
- Roundabouts
- Highway-rail grade crossings

### Safe Intersection Navigation
- Visual search pattern (left-front-right-rear)
- Identifying and selecting best lane position
- Speed management at intersections
- Communication with other drivers
- Stop positions (legal, staggered, safety)

### Driver Responses to Traffic Controls
- Responding to traffic signals (steady, flashing, arrows)
- Responding to regulatory signs at intersections
- Following roadway markings through intersections
- Turn lane usage

### Right of Way at Intersections
- Four-way stops
- Two-way stops
- Uncontrolled intersections (right-of-way rules)
- Emergency vehicle yielding
- Pedestrian right of way
- School bus yielding
- Funeral processions
- School zones and construction zones
- Railroad crossings (controlled and uncontrolled)
- Roundabout yielding procedures
