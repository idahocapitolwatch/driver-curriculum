# Module 2: Basic Maneuvers

**Unit:** 1 - Getting Ready to Drive
**Course:** DRV101 - Driver Education

## Idaho Standards Alignment

| Standard | Benchmark | Description |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| 4.2.1 | Starting Procedures | Describe and demonstrate proper starting procedures |
| 4.2.2 | Steering Control | Describe and demonstrate steering wheel control techniques and when each is used |
| 4.2.3 | Entering/Leaving Roadway | Describe and demonstrate procedures for entering and leaving the roadway |
| 4.2.4 | Acceleration Control | Describe and demonstrate acceleration control |
| 4.2.5 | Braking Control | Describe and demonstrate controlled, threshold, and trail braking control |
| 4.2.6 | 90-Degree Corners | Describe and demonstrate proper entry into and exit out of a 90 degree corner |
| 4.2.7 | Precision Turns | Describe and demonstrate procedures for left and right precision turns from a stopped and moving position |
| 4.2.8 | Backing | Describe and demonstrate procedures for backing straight and while turning |

## Lesson Objectives

1. **Describe steering wheel control techniques and when each is used**
   - Correlations: 4.2.1-4.2.8

2. **Describe procedures for backing straight and while turning**
   - Correlations: 4.2.1-4.2.8

3. **Describe acceleration control**
   - Correlations: 4.2.1-4.2.8

4. **Describe controlled, threshold, and trail braking control**
   - Correlations: 4.2.1-4.2.8

5. **Describe procedures for entering and leaving the roadway**
   - Correlations: 4.2.1-4.2.8

## Key Content Topics

### Steering Techniques
- Hand-over-hand steering
- Push-pull steering
- One-hand steering (when appropriate)
- Hand position on the steering wheel

### Starting and Moving
- Pre-start safety checks
- Engine start procedure
- Smooth acceleration from a stop
- Controlled deceleration

### Acceleration Control
- Smooth, gradual acceleration
- Appropriate speed for conditions
- Speed management in different zones

### Braking Techniques
- Controlled braking (normal stops)
- Threshold braking (maximum stopping without lockup)
- Trail braking (gradual release through turns)
- ABS awareness and proper technique

### Navigating Corners
- Approach speed for 90-degree turns
- Proper entry and exit technique
- Left and right precision turns from stopped position
- Left and right precision turns from moving position

### Backing Maneuvers
- Backing straight
- Backing while turning
- Proper use of mirrors and direct observation
- Speed control while backing

### Entering and Leaving the Roadway
- Merging procedures
- Proper signaling
- Gap selection
- Speed adjustment
