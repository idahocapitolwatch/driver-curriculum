## Key Content Topics

### Gauges and Warning Symbols
- Dashboard warning lights and their meanings
- Fuel gauge, temperature gauge, speedometer, tachometer
- Oil pressure, battery, check engine indicators
- Owner's manual reference for vehicle-specific warnings

### Vehicle Control Devices
- Steering wheel and controls
- Accelerator, brake pedal, parking brake
- Turn signals, headlights, windshield wipers
- Horn, hazard lights, mirrors
- Gear selector / transmission controls

### Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS)
- Blind spot monitoring
- Lane departure warning / lane keeping assist
- Adaptive cruise control
- Automatic emergency braking
- Backup cameras and parking sensors
- Benefits, limitations, and proper use

### Preparing to Drive
- Pre-entry checks (walk around, tire condition, obstacles)
- Seat adjustment, headrest position
- Mirror adjustment (traditional and enhanced/BGE method)
- Seatbelt use for driver and all passengers
- Starting procedures
- Securing the vehicle after driving

### Driving Environments Overview
- Rural characteristics and hazards
- Urban characteristics and hazards
- Introduction to Highway Transportation System
