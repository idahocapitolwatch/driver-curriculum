- 8.1 Driving Within the Highway Transportation System

## Unit Objectives

By the end of this unit, students will be able to:

1. Describe ways to safely share the road with various roadway users
2. Identify various parking configurations
3. Identify safe procedures for lane changes and passing
4. Describe characteristics and hazards of rural driving environments
5. Describe characteristics and hazards of urban driving environments
6. Discuss the safety features and risks involved in freeway driving
7. Discover connections between classroom learning and behind-the-wheel application
