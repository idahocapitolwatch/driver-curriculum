## Lesson Objectives

1. **Describe procedures for left and right precision turns from a stopped and moving position**
   - Correlations: 4.2.1-4.2.8

2. **Describe proper entry into and exit out of a 90-degree corner**
   - Correlations: 4.2.1-4.2.8

3. **Explain the different types of turnabouts**
   - Correlations: 4.4.1, 4.4.2, 4.4.3

4. **Explain the different types of parking**
   - Correlations: 4.5.1-4.5.10

## Key Content Topics

### Precision Turns
- Left turns from a stopped position
- Left turns from a moving position
- Right turns from a stopped position
- Right turns from a moving position
- Visual reference points for turn setup, entry, and exit
- Speed and steering coordination

### 90-Degree Corners
- Proper approach speed
- Correct entry technique
- Maintaining lane position through the turn
- Smooth exit and acceleration

### Turnabouts
- **U-Turn:** When and where legal, execution technique
- **2-Point Turn:** Using a driveway on the right or left
- **3-Point Turn:** Step-by-step procedure, when to use
- Selecting the safest turnabout for the situation
- Checking traffic before and during maneuver

### Parking Maneuvers
- **Angled Parking:** Approach, positioning, pulling in/out
- **Parallel Parking:** Step-by-step procedure, reference points
- **Street/Curb Parking:** Distance from curb, wheel positioning
- **Perpendicular Forward:** Approach and positioning
- **Perpendicular Backing-In:** Mirrors, reference points, technique

### Hill Parking
- **Uphill with curb:** Turn wheels left (away from curb)
- **Uphill without curb:** Turn wheels right (toward shoulder)
- **Downhill with curb:** Turn wheels right (toward curb)
- **Downhill without curb:** Turn wheels right (toward shoulder)
- Setting parking brake on hills

### Restricted Parking Areas
- Fire hydrants
- Crosswalks
- Intersections
- Handicapped spaces
- No parking zones
- Double parking
